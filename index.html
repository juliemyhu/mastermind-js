Mastermind

The objective of the game is to guess the 4-digit code
Hints are given for each number in your guess:
  • 0 - right number, wrong place
  • 1 - right number, right place
You have 10 hints to beat the game! *Note the hints do not tell you which number it is referring to
There are 3 levels of difficulty
  • Easy - numbers are from [0 - 4]
  • Normal - numbers are from [0 - 7]
  • Hard - numbers are from [0 - 9]
All levels can have repeated numbers
Goodluck :)
